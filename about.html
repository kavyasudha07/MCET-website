Institution Overview

Methodist College of Engineering & Technology (MCET) is an autonomous engineering institution located in the heart of Hyderabad, Telangana. The college is affiliated with Osmania University and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It is known for its focus on academic excellence, discipline, and professional development.

Academic Philosophy

The institution follows a learner-centered academic approach that encourages both theoretical understanding and practical application. The curriculum is designed to meet university regulations while incorporating modern teaching methods and emerging technologies. Continuous assessment and structured evaluations help students track their progress and improve learning outcomes.

Autonomous Status

As an autonomous institution, MCET has the flexibility to update syllabi, introduce innovative evaluation techniques, and improve academic delivery. This autonomy allows the college to align its academic framework with industry requirements and global trends, while maintaining regulatory standards.

Faculty and Learning Environment

The college is supported by experienced and qualified faculty members who guide students through academics, projects, and research activities. Regular workshops, seminars, industrial visits, and guest lectures contribute to a dynamic and engaging learning environment.

Student-Centered Development

MCET emphasizes the holistic development of students by promoting participation in technical clubs, professional associations, sports, and cultural activities. Training and placement initiatives focus on employability skills, higher education readiness, and career guidance, helping students prepare for professional success.
