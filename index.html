About the Institution

Methodist College of Engineering & Technology is an autonomous engineering institution located in Hyderabad, Telangana. The college follows academic regulations prescribed by Osmania University and regulatory bodies while maintaining flexibility through autonomy to enhance academic quality.

The institution emphasizes technical education combined with ethical values, professional responsibility, and continuous learning. Students are encouraged to develop strong fundamentals, practical skills, and adaptability required in today’s competitive professional environment.
